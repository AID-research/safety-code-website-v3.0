About

Safer therapies

The Medication Safety Code (MSC) system enables health care providers to make their patients’ medical therapies safer and more effective.

Privacy protection

The MSC is secure and does not require central storage of patient data. Patients have full control over their data.

Expertise

The MSC system was developed at the Medical University of Vienna and has participants and supporters from all over the world.
